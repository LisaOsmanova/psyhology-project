Method of Work: Client-Centered Therapy

It is a certain type of interaction between the counselor and the client, allowing the client to reach an understanding of himself or herself and to take constructive steps to improve the quality of life.

Psychotherapy is an opportunity to freely express and explore one's feelings and thoughts. What does my pain, fear, dissatisfaction, irritability, depression say? Why isn't it going away and getting harder?

Psychotherapy is about you, your aspirations in life, and how, without wanting or noticing, you complicate it to achieve your goal.
